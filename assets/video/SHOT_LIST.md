## Editing notes

- Keep one consistent palette across art, mocks, and product: near-black `#0d0a07`, orange `#e8742c`, green = good, red = constrained.
- All frames have darker bottom space reserved for subtitles/lower-thirds.
- Frames are 1024×~683 (3:2). For a 1920×1080 timeline, fill with a slow zoom or add a blurred letterbox; the reserved bottom space keeps focal content safe.
